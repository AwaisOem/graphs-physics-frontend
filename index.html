How To Use

From the first column, select the desired option: ACE, DSCOR, WIND
From the second column, select the type of chart you want to generate
In the third column, enter the starting year
In the fourth column, enter the starting day of the year
In the fifth column, enter the ending year
In the sixth column, enter the ending day

Data Ranges

The data of ACE is from 1999 to 2020
The data of DSCOR is from 2017 to 2018
The data of Wind is from 1995 to 2022
